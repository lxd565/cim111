About Us

SUGAR! was founded in late April of 2018. Founder and head baker, Lexi baked her dreams come true. She started baking ever since she was young and knew this was her passion. Finally got the oppertunity to make it a reality after years of training and baking. SUGAR! is the first bakery to be opened by Lexi and she is hopoing to expand to multiple locations in the FLorida area by 2020.
